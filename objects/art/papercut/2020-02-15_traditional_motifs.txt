Traditional Chinese Papercut Artwork
Artist: Cheuk Kit (Philip) Chung
Date: February 15, 2020

Title: Harmony of Dragons and Phoenix
Medium: Red paper, hand-cut
Dimensions: 30cm x 30cm

Description:
This intricate papercut artwork features traditional Chinese motifs of dragons and phoenix in circular harmony. The piece demonstrates the delicate balance between masculine and feminine elements in Chinese mythology, executed through precise hand-cutting techniques.
