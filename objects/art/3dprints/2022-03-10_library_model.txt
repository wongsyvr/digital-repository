3D Printed Architectural Model
Designer: Cheuk Kit (Philip) Chung
Date: March 10, 2022

Project: Modern Community Library
Scale: 1:87 (HO scale)

Technical Specifications:
- Printer: Prusa i3 MK3S+
- Material: PLA (White)
- Print Time: 48 hours
- Resolution: 0.1mm layer height

Description:
This architectural model represents a modern community library design that combines traditional reading spaces with digital media centers. The design features a central atrium with natural lighting and modular study spaces around the perimeter.
